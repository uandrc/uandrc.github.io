Why "boring" HTML pages are good

You have likely noticed a pattern. All of these university websites are plainer than a slice of white bread.

When I first noticed this pattern of plainness, I was confused. Why would universities with the ability, money, and resources stoop to such low website-viewing standards? Surely a couple decades would be ample time to make their website at least a smidge prettier, no?

However, while many universities indeed have ability, money, and resources, their websites' questionable stylistic choices are rooted in good reasons.
