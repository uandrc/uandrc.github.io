No One Cares

In general, no one cares about you. They care what you have to offer to them. This applies to:
  • Code: Users don't care how elegant your functions are. They don't even know they exist. They only care whether what you've made works.
  • Journalism: Inverted pyramid structure.
  • YouTube:
People are result-oriented. This is not necessarily a bad thing - at the end of the day, if something doesn't work, it is a problem.
