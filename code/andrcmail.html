AndrcMail

AndrcMail is a lightweight, minimalist email client written in Java Swing.

Setup

  1. Download the AndrcMail file below.
  2. Open a Google Chrome window. Sign in to the Google account you want AndrcMail to access email for.
  3. Open Google App Passwords in the window you opened in Step 2.
  4. Under the 'App Name' field, enter AndrcMail. Next, press the 'Create' button.
  5. After you press 'Create', a generated password will be displayed. Save this password in a safe place for the next step, you will need it!
  6. Run the AndrcMail app. Login using your full Gmail username (e.g., "john_doe@gmail.com") and the Google App Password saved from the previous step.

I Forgot my AndrcMail password. What do I do?

Don't worry! Simply repeat steps 2 to 5 to create a new Google App Password you can use to login to your AndrcMail account.
