Stretch List

I do the below list of stretches when I have the time. It daresay it has improved my life.

Dynamic Stretches

  • Neck rolls (left/right)
  • Arm Swings/Arm Circles (forwards/backwards)
  • Wrist and Ankle rolls (left/right)
  • Leg Swings (side to side, front to back)
  • Butterfly stretch

Static Stretches

  • Cross-Body Arm Stretch
  • Triceps Stretch
  • Toe touches (standing/sitting)
  • Standing Quad Stretch
  • Standing Knee Hugs
  • Standing Lunge
  • Side Lunge
    • the one where you link hands behind back and the other one like but make fist with both hands the sitting leg one where you cross one leg over the other (l/r sides)
For the stretches marked as static, hold the stretch for 15 to 25 seconds. For dynamic stretches, do 12 repetitions of the stretch.
