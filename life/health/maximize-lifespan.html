Maximize your Lifespan

Ten of the most effective ways to improve your health and quality of life.

The below list is ordered based on avoiding the leading causes of preventable death.
  1. Abstain from substances of abuses (e.g., smoking, alcohol, etc.).
  2. Exercise regularly, reduce sitting and screen time.
  3. Eat a healthy diet.
  4. Get 7-9 hours of sleep nightly.
  6. Have a sense of purpose: do something you love daily.
